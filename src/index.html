National Collegiate Wrestling Team at UC San Diego

About Us

The National Collegiate Wrestling Team at UC San Diego is dedicated to promoting the sport of wrestling and fostering a competitive, supportive environment for student-athletes. We welcome wrestlers of all skill levels to join our team, train hard, and represent UCSD at collegiate competitions. Join us to build strength, discipline, and lifelong friendships!

Support the UCSD Wrestling Club

We're a student-run club dedicated to growing the sport of wrestling on campus. Your support helps us travel, compete, and represent UC San Diego across the nation.
