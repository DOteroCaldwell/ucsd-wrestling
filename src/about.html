About National Collegiate Wrestling Team at UC San Diego

The National Collegiate Wrestling Team at UC San Diego is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting the sport of wrestling on campus and beyond. Our team welcomes athletes of all skill levels, from seasoned competitors to those new to the sport, fostering an inclusive and supportive environment where everyone can grow and excel. We believe in the values of discipline, teamwork, and perseverance, both on and off the mat.

Throughout the academic year, our team participates in regional and national competitions, representing UC San Diego with pride and determination. We offer regular practices, skill-building clinics, and opportunities for members to connect with experienced coaches and alumni. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond competition, as we strive to build lasting friendships and a strong sense of community among our members.

Whether you're looking to compete at a high level or simply want to stay active and learn a new sport, the UCSD Wrestling Team provides the resources and support you need. Join us as we continue to grow the wrestling community at UC San Diego and make a positive impact both on campus and in the broader wrestling world.

Meet the Coaches

Head Coach Andrew Cook

Andrew Cook brings years of collegiate wrestling experience to UC San Diego, having coached and competed at the national level. His coaching philosophy emphasizes technical mastery, mental toughness, and building a supportive team culture. Coach Cook is dedicated to helping athletes reach their full potential both on and off the mat.

Coach Stosh Sitkowski

Stosh Sitkowski is known for his energetic coaching style and commitment to athlete development. With a background in both wrestling and sports education, Coach Sitkowski focuses on skill-building, conditioning, and fostering a positive environment for all team members. He is passionate about growing the sport and mentoring new wrestlers.
